How I got my start

I first became interested in coding when I got my 3d printer. I was in the process of adding a series of upgrades. In order to get the printer where I wanted it to be, I had to crack open the firmware and customize it. A lot of questions and a few sleepless nights later and I am still using that same complied firmware. That was 2 years ago.

Since my first encounter with code, I had been thinking of the differnt options for breaking into the field. While asking around, my wife's friend mentioned she had attented a program at Grand Circus and had nothing but good things to say about it. Not just about the learning experience but the benefits from having atteneded here. It takes me a bit to finalize my big life decisions but am very confident in this new journey.
